I am a PhD student at the Department of Psychiatry and Psychotherapy at Philipps-Universität Marburg. My work is situated at the interface of computational psychiatry, natural language processing (NLP), and clinical neuroscience.
My research focuses on the automated analysis of spontaneous speech to identify latent linguistic markers of psychopathology. I investigate latent linguistic parameters in affective and psychotic disorders using automated speech analysis, structural MRI, diffusion MRI, and advanced statistical and machine-learning approaches.
